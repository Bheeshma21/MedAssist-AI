Hypertension

Hypertension is persistently elevated blood pressure.

Symptoms:
- Often asymptomatic
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Blurred vision

Risk factors:
- Obesity
- Smoking
- Diabetes
- Family history

Complications:
- Stroke
- Heart failure
- Kidney disease
- Heart attack

Common medications:
- Ramipril
- Amlodipine
- Losartan
